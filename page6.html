Go beyond 160 characters with
Prime Message SMS attachments

For the first time ever, sm solutions tech brings email-like attachment functionality to your high engagement bulk SMS campaigns. Now, share files via SMS just the way you do it with email.

Attach files to your SMS in just 2 clicks: While composing your SMS, just click on attachments  and select the file from your computer. sm solutions tech securely hosts this file and inserts it as a clickable link/URL in the body of your text message.

Works better than email
SMS has outperformed Email for years, in terms of both open rates and response rates. The reach for your attachments is 5X higher than Email when you share them via SMS.

Advanced click tracking!
Gain valuable insights into customer behavior by tracking how many clicked the attachment, who clicked along with mobile number, time, device characteristics & more.

How can your business use SMS attachments?

Image

Attach a JPEG/PNG in your SMS and convey more in 160 characters. It’s easy and you don’t have to worry about uploading, hosting them and generating short URLs.

Brochures

Handing out flyers on the street or directly posting them is typically expensive and have low response rates. Attach PDF brochures to an SMS and see response rates hit the roof!

Presentations

Share your powerpoint or pdf presentations via SMS whether it’s a weekly/monthly updates, business performance report or a simple product intro deck for quick review.
